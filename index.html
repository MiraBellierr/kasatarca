OUR STORY

Welcome to Kasat Arca, where we transform recycled materials into extraordinary art sculptures. We specialize in custom orders, from huge landscape sculptures to house decorations. Our creations have been showcased in various exhibitions, including the prestigious Rainforest World Music Festival in Damai, Kuching, Sarawak (2024)
